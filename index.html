Gluten-Free Sugar Cookies

Prep Time: 10 Min
Cook Time: 10 Min
Total Time: 20 Min

These buttery sweet cookies have a soft vanilla flavor, a crisp exterior, and a soft and chewy middle, just like any good gluten free sugar cookie should have.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups almond flour
  • 1 cup tapioca flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • Pinch sea salt
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • Raw sugar, optional

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350° F.
  2. Add almond flour, tapioca flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt to a bowl.
  3. Whisk together until combined.
  4. Add in maple syrup, vanilla, and softened butter. Using a heavy spoon, mix the dough all together until fully combined.
  5. Roll dough into balls and place on baking sheet about 2 inches apart.
  6. Bake for 10 minutes.

Nut Free Basil Pesto

Prep Time: 5 Min
Cook Time: None
Total Time: 5 Min

We whipped up a bright and flavorful basil pesto that doesnâ€™t use pine nuts so that everyone get can enjoy it.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups packed basil
  • 2 cups packed baby kale
  • 1/3 cup parmesan, grated
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 garlic clove
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Hot water, as needed

Directions

  1. Add all of the ingredients except the olive oil to a food processor or a high speed blender. Pulse until finely chopped. With the motor running, drizzle in the olive oil. If needed, you can add in a tablespoon of hot water to make a smooth pesto. Taste and adjust seasoning.
  2. Use immediately or store for up to 1 week in a tightly sealed container.

Watermelon Salad

Prep Time: 15 Min
Cook Time: None
Total Time: 15 Min

Sweet watermelon garnished with toasted almonds, mint, sliced red onion, and a sweet basil ginger dressing.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium watermelon, cubed
  • 1/3 cup slivered almonds, toasted
  • 1/2 red onion, finely sliced
  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • Dash fine pink sea salt
  • 1 to 2 tbsp fresh mint, chopped
  • 1 1/2 tbsp raw honey
  • 1 tbsp water
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 2 tbsp fresh basil

Directions

  1. In a bowl place cubed watermelon.
  2. Drizzle olive oil and sea salt over it.
  3. In a skillet over medium heat, toast almonds.
  4. Assemble the salad by adding your sliced onion, almonds, and mint leaves.
  5. In a blender, blend remaining dressing ingredients until smooth and drizzle over salad.
  6. Serve immediately.
